(the truth hurts) my personal history: Born in 1975, the 29th of November. I lived in Roswell, New Mexico for 3 years. (Alien sightings, yes) Came to Lubbock, Texas in 1978. I really want to live in California. But they say you can write where you are at. Recently, I am thinking of moving to Las Vegas, Nevada. Who knows what the future holds? Recently, I decided on Arizona to live. I am going to check it out in October. So I will move after that. So 2005 seems like the change of Danny. I'll keep you informed.

I went to many schools here in Lubbock, Texas. High School was Coronado. Graduated in 1994. Went to College for a year and a half. I recently went back in 2012. I am still working on finally getting a degree. Sometimes I wish I should have went back when I was working at the movie theater during 1998. I started working there in 1996. I got my education in writing screenplays by watching movie commentaries and reading filmmaking and screenwriting books that I bought at the bookstore and online. I have a lot of books on filmmaking. I am excited with getting back into it slowly with more story ideas. Hopefully, I can post them up here on my website.
